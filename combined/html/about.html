Our Inspiration

Toronto is a crowded city with huge amounts of traffic. Everyday, over 2 million people commute to work, and possibly would expose to covid-19. We want to provide a one-shot solution to help people stay away from risks from infection, and help people make better commute decisions.

What does it do?

    Customized and refined for Toronto

    Fine tuned Toronto covid data aren't readily available before we made it.  Our data visualization and algorithms are more informative and straightforward than simple charts. 

    Always available

    We use APIs from various sources and recommendation systems develop on our own to get up-to-date information about the covid.

    Customizable

    Our data visualization, map systems, and recommendation system are incorporated with well-designed query systems for Toronto.  With APIs and relevant data provided, we can apply our design to every city in the world. 

    Lightweight

    For more reliable performance and effective presentation of features, we choose to use lightweight frameworks such as Leaflet.js and jQuery in our design.  

    Fast

    Data is preprocessed and pulled only when needed.  

    Trackable Sources

    All data come from reliable and trackable sources.
